International Footprint

Our strong footprint in the Middle East, Far East and South Asia reinforces our vision to be one of the leading international financial institutions in the world.

We have branches in Singapore and Qatar, representative offices in Hong Kong and India, and a subsidiary in Libya. With a growing International Footprint and a strong business relationship network, FGB enjoys extended geographical coverage for Debt Markets and Syndications as well as Global Transaction Services.

This international footprint has expanded rapidly during the last five years to six international locations, vigorously supplementing our overall growth strategy and wholesale business expansion plans.

A strong financial performance track record, robust shareholding and extended geographical presence put us in a unique position to continue our growth and success by playing an enhanced role as an active international financial institution.

Today we are recognized as a world-class organization committed to maximizing value for shareholders, customers and employees. Besides being awarded several regional accolades and awards, we have been ranked the 3rd most powerful company in the UAE and 6th leading bank in Forbes 'Top 500 in the Arab World List'.

FGB carries a Long Term (IDR) Rating by Fitch of A+, an A2 Long Term Deposit Rating from Moody's, A+ Long Term Foreign Currency Rating from Capital Intelligence and AAA Long Term Financial Institution Rating from RAM Ratings of Malaysia.
